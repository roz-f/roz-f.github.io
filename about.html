About Retostella

Retostella Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young people through entrepreneurship and digital literacy. We believe that access to knowledge, skills, and mentorship can transform lives and create sustainable opportunities for individuals and communities.


Our mission is to equip the next generation with the tools they need to succeed in an ever-changing world. Through tailored programs, workshops and mentorship, we nurture creativity, innovation and business acumen in aspiring entrepreneurs and digital professionals.


Our Vision: To create a future where every young person has the knowledge and confidence to leverage technology and entrepreneurship for economic and personal growth. Through strategic education and mentorship, we aim to bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity.

Expert Mentorship

Our dedicated mentors provide guidance and real-world insights, helping young people develop skills in entrepreneurship and digital literacy.

Entrepreneurship Training

We equip young minds with business and financial literacy skills, empowering them to turn ideas into sustainable ventures.

Digital Literacy

We provide training in essential digital skills, ensuring young individuals are prepared for the modern workforce and digital economy.

RetOStella Foundation is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and digital literacy among youths, equipping them with the skills to succeed in the modern economy.
