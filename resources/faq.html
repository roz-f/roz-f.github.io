Frequently Asked Questions

Retostella Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young individuals through entrepreneurship, digital literacy, mentorship, and training programs. Our goal is to equip people with the skills and opportunities needed to thrive in today’s economy.

Our programs are designed for young people, entrepreneurs, and individuals looking to develop digital skills, business acumen, and professional growth. Whether you're a beginner or someone looking to enhance your expertise, our programs cater to all levels.

You can apply for our mentorship program by visiting our website and filling out the application form on the Mentorship page. We match mentees with experienced professionals based on interests, career goals, and expertise.

Our digital literacy program covers coding, digital marketing, content creation, financial technology, data analytics, and cybersecurity. These courses provide practical skills that enhance employability and career growth.

If you are passionate about guiding young individuals in their personal and professional development, you can apply to be a mentor or volunteer through our website. We provide orientation and training to ensure impactful mentorship.

Donations can be made directly through our website. We accept online payments, bank transfers, and sponsorships. Your contributions help us expand our impact and reach more young individuals in need.
