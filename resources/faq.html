Frequently Asked Questions

Retostella Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young people through Entrepreneurship & Digital Literacy. We provide training, mentorship, and resources to help youth build businesses, navigate the digital world, and create sustainable opportunities for themselves.

"Retostella" is a coined name from Osaretin Obayagbona and Stella Obayagbona, symbolizing our commitment to family values, mentorship, and youth empowerment through education and skill development.

We offer workshops, mentorship sessions, and hands-on training in business development, digital marketing, e-commerce, freelancing, and financial literacy.

Our programs are open to young individuals, entrepreneurs, and anyone looking to develop digital and business skills. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced professional, there’s something for everyone.

We offer both online and in-person training sessions. Physical training locations vary depending on partnerships and community outreach programs. Details about upcoming sessions can be found on our website and social media.

The duration of our programs depends on the specific training. Some workshops last a few days, while comprehensive programs can span several weeks or months. The timeline for each program will be stated during enrollment.
